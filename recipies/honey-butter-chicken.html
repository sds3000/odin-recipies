Honey Butter Chicken recipie

OMG!!! You've never had chicken like this! Crispy,
sweet, and savory. It's almost a forbidden fruit.
If you don't like this you didn't make it right. Don't cheap out on the ingridients for max flavor

    Ingridients

  • 2lbs chicke thighs
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 ginger slices, 1 inch each
  • frying oil
  • parsley flakes

    • Batter ingridients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup corn starch
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup ice water, or as needed

    • Honey Butter Sauce

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

    Recipie

  1. In a medium bowl combine white sugar, salt, garlic, ginger, and rosemary together.
    Cut the chicken into 1 inch pieces, put the chicken in and cover.
    Leave in refrigerator overnight.
  2. Heat the oil to 365.
  3. Drain the chicken and put the batter into chicken and mx by hand(dont touch the oil)
  4. drop chicken into the oil and fry until golden brown.
  5. take out and let rest for 10 min
  6. drop chicken back in until darker brown
  7. next in a sauce pan put 1 stick of butter in and heat until liquid
  8. combine the remaining ingridients of the honey butter sauce
  9. after it begins to bubble, throw the fried chicken in and mix until all pieces are
    drenched in the sauce
  10. eat until stomach burst!!
