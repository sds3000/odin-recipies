Butter Steak Recipie

Ingredients
  • 1 Ribeye 1.5 in thick
  • salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
Recipie
  1. Salt your steak and let it rest in your fridge over night
  2. next day take out the steak and cut of some of the fat to use for fying the steak(you can also use your regular cooking oil)
  3. preheat your oven to 225 and cook in your oven until it has an internal temp of 115
  4. after letting your steak rest for 10 minutes pepper steak
  5. now fry your steak until it gets that good sear
  6. now add your butter, rosemary and garlic to your pan and let the butter melt and absorb those flavors
  7. cut your steak into 1/4 in pieces and drizzle the butter over the steak
  8. enjoy!!
